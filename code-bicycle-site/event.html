Annual event: "Cycle the Continental Divide"

Welcome!

Welcome to The Great Divide Mountain Bike Route (GDMBR) page! It's the most recognized and important off-pavement cycling route in the United States, if not the world. The route crisscrosses the Continental Divide from north to south starting in Banff, Alberta, Canada and finishing at the US/Mexico border in Antelope Wells, New Mexico.

This 2018 year

If you are planning to join our team it's good to know the following info:

dates: 30 July - 5 September

route: Banff, Alberta, Canada - Antelope Wells, New Mexico

costs: $600-900 + extras

basic requirements: lot of planning as it pertains to proper weather gear and resupply, bicycle, helmet, backpack, tent, first aid kit, water, food and map.
